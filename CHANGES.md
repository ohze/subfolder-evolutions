##### 2.8.1
+ We don't follow play versioning anymore.
+ Support play 2.6.x, 2.7.x, 2.8.x, scala 2.11, 2.12, 2.13

+ Update sbt & use [sbt-devops](https://github.com/ohze/sbt-devops)
+ Binary & source compatible with 2.8.0 & 2.6.7
